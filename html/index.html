Prevention Tips From Dr. Nitin Kumar


How To Protect Yourself?


1.Increase bone density with Calcium to diminish the risk of fractures. Always ensure that you incorporate enough calcium in your diet. Low calcium would only mean early bone loss and reduced bone density.

2.RDA (Recommended Dietary Allowance) is 1,000 milligrams (mg) / day for men between the ages of 51 and 70 and adults between the ages of 19 and 50 RDA for women over the age of 50 and men over the age of 70 years is 1,200 milligrams (mg) / day

3.Perform weight bearing and strength exercises regularly. Your daily routine must include physical activities such as jogging, climbing stairs, etc. to increase bone strength and density and slow down bone loss.
